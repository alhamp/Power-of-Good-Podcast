About Power Of Good

The Power of Good podcast is a series that highlights the work of people doing great things, caring things, often life-saving things for other people. These are the altruists, the optimists, and the social entrepreneurs among us – those helping others across backgrounds, politics, and geography in compassionate and creative ways.

how to listen:

jake Murray

Creator of Power for Good Podcast
Jake Murray is a nonprofit leader, with over 25 years of experience in higher education, PreK-12 education, public health, and human service. He’s passionate about strengthening communities, and promoting education and career opportunities for learners from all backgrounds. In addition to producing and hosting the Power of Good podcast, Jake has served: on the faculty of the Boston University School of Education; as executive director of the Aspire Institute at Wheelock College; as a city planner for the City of Cambridge; and as the program officer of the Harvard University Children's Initiative. He lives in the Brookline MA with his wife and two sons.
